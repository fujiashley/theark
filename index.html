About

Why name our house "The Ark"? Well it's a reminder of the biblical story of Noah's Ark or rather the Ark God provided to save Noah and his family from the great flood.

Likewise this humble abode is God's blessing to us - a place of shelter, warmth, peace for the whole family.

A lot of effort and resources went into building this home which was not possible without the support of our parents. We are greatly indepted to them.
